Who We Are

PhotMedia Ghana is pioneering the digital transformation of public transportation in Ghana. Our innovative USSD-based mTicketing platform enables passengers to book bus tickets using any mobile phone, even basic feature phones.

Founded in 2019, we've grown from a small startup to Ghana's leading transport technology company, serving over 500 transport operators and processing millions of bookings annually.
